Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website design services, pricing, and process.

How much does a website cost?

Website costs vary depending on complexity, features, and customization. Basic informational websites start at affordable rates for small businesses, while more complex e-commerce or feature-rich sites cost more. We provide custom quotes based on your specific needs. Contact us for a free consultation.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on project scope and complexity. A typical small business website takes 2-6 weeks from start to launch. This includes design, development, content optimization, and testing. We'll provide a specific timeline with your project quote.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive website maintenance services including security updates, content management, plugin updates, and technical support. Regular maintenance keeps your site secure, fast, and ranking well in search engines.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. All websites we design are fully responsive and mobile-optimized. Your site will look perfect on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. This is essential since over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices.

Do you handle website redesigns?

Yes! If your website looks outdated or isn't performing well, we offer professional redesign services. We'll modernize your design, improve functionality, enhance performance, and optimize for better conversion rates.

Will my website be optimized for search engines (SEO)?

Yes. All our websites are built with SEO best practices including fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, clean code structure, meta tags, and schema markup. This gives your site a strong foundation for ranking well in Google and other search engines.

Can I update my website content myself?

Yes! We can build your site with a content management system (CMS) that lets you easily update text, images, and content without technical knowledge. Alternatively, we can handle all updates for you as part of our maintenance service.

Do you provide website hosting?

We focus on design and development. For hosting, we recommend reliable providers, or we can help you set up hosting with a partner. We ensure your site is compatible with all major hosting platforms.

How can I get started?

Simply reach out to us via our contact page or request a free quote. We'll discuss your project needs, answer any questions, and provide a customized proposal. There's no obligation—we're here to help!
